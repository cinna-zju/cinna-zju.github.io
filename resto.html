digiCrous

INTRO

Crous is a French organization that provides meals for students. But there are some problems. DigiCrous is a project to improve the dinning experience by adding some digital support.

This is the project of Advanced Design of Interaction System.

TIME

Feb 2019



ROLE

Interview

Prototype
Wireframe

User research

We interviewed eight users using critical incident interview and made a questionnaire which received 24 responses.

Here are some results from the questionnaire.

From the interview and questionnaire results, we found that there were 4 common problems.

  • Waiting too long before getting meals
  • Students don't know the daily menu
  • The ingredients of the food is not clear
  • No balance reminder

    • The main problem was that students could't get these information unless they went to the restaurant. So we decided to design a mobile app to provide necessary information to students and help students track what they ate.

    Brainstorming

    To help Ming, we designed a function that showing today's menu and basic information about food. Then I designed a participatory design activity to figure out what is the basic information for food.

    This technique is to bring users and designers together for collaborative exploration of ideas.

    Activity: linking

    Users will see some pictures of different kinds of food and link whatever they think about to the food and group some food by color according to their feelings.

    The result was similiar to what we expected. Users cared about ingredients, nutrition and flavor. But using color to group food was not a good idea, since users had different understanding about colors.

    Prototyping

    When users clicked one of the restaurant, they could see today's food in different catogories.

    When users clicked one of the food, they could see the details including name, nutritions, ingredients, allergic information and some comments.

    He can filter gluten out using the filter function.

    Evaluation

    Then we thought there should be two types of filter. First, filter always applied for allergics, vegan, etc. The second filter is for temporary use.

    The first type of filter was like user settings. Users can set it at the first time using this app.
